Our Story

Mario's Pizzeria began in 1983 when Mario Rossi immigrated from Naples with nothing but his nonna's secret dough recipe and a dream. What started as a small neighborhood joint has grown into a beloved institution, but we've never forgotten our roots.

Our wood-fired brick oven was imported piece by piece from Italy and assembled by master craftsmen. Every pizza is still made using traditional techniques passed down through four generations of the Rossi family.
